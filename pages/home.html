About Birch Haven

Birch Haven is a rescue & rehabilitation center, that personally rescues many diffrent canine breeds that have been abandoned, abused, neglected and otherwise mistreated. We also try to promote responsible pet ownership through Humane Education, Training, Socialization and Community Public Awareness. We are also involved in Therapy work.
