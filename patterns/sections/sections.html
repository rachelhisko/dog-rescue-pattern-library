About Birch Haven

Birch Haven is a rescue & rehabilitation center, that personally rescues Great Danes/other Giant Canine Breeds and Equine animals that have been abandoned, abused, neglected and otherwise mistreated. We also try to promote responsible pet ownership through Humane Education, Training, Socialization and Community Public Awareness. We are also involved in Therapy work.
